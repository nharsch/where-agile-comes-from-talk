From Deming to Agile

1940s–50s: Deming — Statistical Quality Control, PDCA
1950s–70s: Toyota Production System — JIT, Kanban, Kaizen
1980s: Total Quality Management — customer focus, CI
1984: Theory of Constraints — bottlenecks, throughput
1990s: Lean Manufacturing — value stream, waste reduction
1986–90s: Six Sigma — DMAIC, defect reduction
2001: Agile — Scrum, XP, Kanban, DevOps
